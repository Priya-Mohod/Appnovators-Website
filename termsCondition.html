SatUsh’s Mission

Many people in offices find themselves with untouched food in their tiffins and offer it to colleagues who don’t have food. However, in many cases, if there is no one nearby interested, this food goes to waste. While the food wasted from one tiffin might seem insignificant, when accumulated across offices or an entire city, it becomes a serious issue.

SatietyApp aims to reduce this problem by providing a platform for individuals to share untouched homemade food with colleagues who need it, thereby reducing food waste. The platform facilitates this food-sharing process but does not take any responsibility for the quality, condition, or safety of the food exchanged.
